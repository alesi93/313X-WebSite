Industries

Finance

More trust between the customer and the bank is at the heart of financial services. People trust their financial institutions to protect their financial interests, their personal identity and financial information. Because of the sensitivity of the data they hold and the services they provide, the financial services industry has long been the focus of cybersecurity regulations and audits.

Those regulations mandate strong protections to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of data and transactions and the availability of their services. So, just as financial institutions require good data to provide financial services, they also require good data to manage cybersecurity risk. 313X can provide essential data, analytics, and workflow necessary to easily understand and act on cybersecurity risk.

Healthcare

Data is at the heart of healthcare and is intimately personal to patients and highly targeted by cybercriminals. Because healthcare regulatory and industry bodies recognize the need to protect patient data, the Department of Health and Human Services on EUA codifies data protection regulations in the HIPAA Security Rule.

The Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS) recommends that healthcare organizations adopt the NIST Cybersecurity Framework to help ensure good cybersecurity risk outcomes. And 313X can act as your trusted cyber security partner, giving you the confidence to balance seemingly conflicting or difficult demands for increased resilience and ongoing innovation to achieve a secure competitive advantage.

Defense

We cannot talk about defense, without talking about cyber. National security depends on the strength of the nation’s defense, of which defense contractors are an essential part. Defense contractors provide expertise spanning areas such as weapons manufacturing, research and development, intelligence, data analytics, and personnel management and support.

The confidentiality, integrity, and availability of these services and the related data are critical. Unauthorized disclosure of weapons or intelligence data could yield valuable advantages to adversaries. Disclosure of something as simple as personnel information could expose military professionals to targeted attacks.

Energy

The world counts on the energy sector to power their lives - whether it be petroleum products for their cars, natural gas to heat their homes, or electricity to power lights and computing infrastructure. Keeping the power on requires good operations and engineering. It also requires good cybersecurity for your own enterprise and the third-party providers that you depend on.

Asking your team to fully understand the ever-changing global cyber risks across the increasingly interconnected supply chain is a tough ask. Balancing operational needs and investment while mitigating cyber threats in this landscape requires expert and practical knowledge. That's where 313X can help.

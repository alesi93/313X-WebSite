Incident Response

Get help from our skilled Incident Response team operating globally to ensure rapid and thorough containment, remediation and recovery of the most damaging cyber attacks.

High-level steps of Incident Response

  • Network traffic analysis: Under the guidance of 313X experts your IT personnel implements Threat Hunting Framework for network traffic monitoring and suspicious behavior detection missed by signature-based cybersecurity systems

  • Forensic analysis: 313X specialists conduct express forensic analysis of workstations and servers compromised by cybercriminals to identify the initial attack vector, applied tools and techniques as well as exploited vulnerabilities

  • Malware analysis: Our certified malware analysts perform basic or advanced static and dynamic analysis of malicious code discovered during an investigation to determine other affected assets in the environment and prevent further intrusions

Our post-incident deliverables

  • Clear network infrastructure:We gather all necessary information for creating a list of Indicators of Compromise, write YARA-rules to clear your enterprise’s network from the infiltration

  • Investigative report with attacker profile: Our experts explore the anatomy of the attack — how attackers gained a foothold and moved laterally and vertically inside your organization to steal confidential data

  • Remediation report and recommendations: After analysis, we prepare a detailed report on how to adjust your security architecture and processes to strengthen your security posture

Cyber Investigation

Combating computer-based, financial, corporate crimes of varied size and complexity.

Why our investigators?

  • Individual approach by special project team: Every investigation is different and may require a different route through the process. 313X’s investigations are conducted by a diverse project team consisting of e-Discovery and Forensic analysts, economic security, financial audit, corporate law specialists, financial crime experts

  • Collaboration with law enforcement agencies: As technology reaches into nearly all areas of our lives, law enforcement officers are challenged to maintain the skills and tools needed to conduct thorough investigations. Our experts work closely with international police in combating various types of crimes — from computer-based crimes to murders and missing people

  • Proprietary technologies for criminal detection: 313X’s investigation department has adopted a proactive approach and developed a set of technologies for identifying groups or individuals who are assessed as being involved in ongoing criminal activity. We accelerate investigations using in-house tools for pattern analysis, network analysis, and tactical profiling

What types of crimes do we handle?

High-tech crime investigations are the core area of our activity. We conduct a wide variety of investigations, including:

  • Data theft: Competitive espionage, phishing, intellectual property breach, theft of trade secrets, credentials and other sensitive information

  • Insider attacks: Abuse of authority, competitive espionage, critical infrastructure damaging, data leakage, account takeover

  • Information wars: Extortion, reputational damage, defamation, harassment and identity theft

  • Flood attacks: Overload of communication channels (emails, phone calls, social messengers)

  • DoS/DDoS attacks: Competitive and targeted attacks to overload web services and network

  • Critical infrastructure attacks: Attacks on water supply, transport infrastructure, power grids control systems

  • Malware attacks: Malware creation, proliferation and control, botnets, ransomware and spyware

  • Financial crimes: Cryptocurrency theft, online‑banking, mobile-banking, email fraud, ATMs, card processing, SWIFT, payment gateways

What you get after the investigation

The results of our investigations include:

  • Detailed threat actor profile: We explore the anatomy of the attack and threat actor’s infrastructure, identify the mechanisms and recreate the sequence of events.

  • Professional evidence collection for court: 313X investigators collect all digital evidence in compliance with legal requirements and prepare required documentation for presenting evidence correctly in court.

  • Law enforcement consultation: Our investigators have sound knowledge of the relevant law and provide full cycle consultation support to lawyers and different enforcement agencies on every stage of investigation.

Digital Forensics and Malware Analysis

Evidence that fights back. Our forensic and malware analyses have helped victims of infamous hacker groups come out as victors in courts of law. Our experts can do the same for your company.

Our high-expertise services

  • Digital Forensics: Forensic examinations, initiated independently or by law enforcement agencies ensure that all the information needed is correctly seized and copied

  • Malware Analysis: We examine malware while taking into consideration confidentiality requirements, for example by blocking any network interactions with harmful programs. We provide a comprehensive diagnosis and recommendations on further steps without additional risks

  • Collection of digital evidence: Correctly collected and documented digital evidence forms the basis for further investigation or case preparation for court proceedings

How we can help you?

  • Provide accurate information about the incident: We reconstruct incident timelines and provide insights into the motivation behind the attack and the level of employee involvement 

  • Help build a strong court case: Prepare your legal team and build your case for court

  • Detect residual malicious code in your network: We help identify the scope of compromise correctly, detect all the affected devices, and clear the network. It is crucial to be certain that the incident response team did not overlook anything to prevent further attacks

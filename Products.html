Why 313X | TPCRM?

You can't protect what you can't see.

Your enterprise relies on third parties to fulfill essential services. You also count on them to protect the security of the data and the availability of the services with which they are entrusted. 313X | TPCRM provides you comprehensive vendor security monitoring, making it easy for you to understand your risks and enabling you to efficiently engage with your vendors to take action on the risks to achieve good risk outcomes.

Make Better Informed Vendor Selections

It’s your risk, so you have to select vendors that are going to protect your risk interests well. 313X enhances your new vendor selection process by providing you readily available objective assessments for each of the parties you are evaluating. Get the intimate risk performance assessments you need to efficiently manage your third-party risk and compare the overall vendor ratings, or dive into the details to a better and immersive comprehention. Either way, you are making better decisions. And you are doing it faster - at the speed of business.

Conduct Assessments More Efficiently

When your analyst engages with a third-party, they need to get the job done well and quickly, because there are mountains of others to assess. 313X | TPCRM enables your analysts to engage with vendors more efficiently, providing the IT environment details and objective cybersecurity assessment before they even engage. If their questionnaire responses look good, and the 313X | TPCRM assessment looks clean, you have a strong basis for wrapping the assessment quickly. And where 313X | TPCRM calls out issues, your analysts know the areas on which to focus.

Prioritize Your Vendor Portfolio

So many vendors to assess, yet so few resources to get the job done. 313X | TPCRM provides you data-driven insights that enable you to more intelligently prioritize your vendor risk assessments and better manage your third-party risk assessment priorities better. With 313X | TPCRM performance ratings, you have a strong indicator of vendor performance, enabling you to prioritize assessment resources towards the lower performing vendors and away from the high performing vendors.

Realize Better Risk Outcomes

Performing third-party assessments without objective data puts you at a huge disadvantage, leaving you only the ability to review unsubstantiated questionnaire answers. 313X | TPCRM objectively verifies vendor cybersecurity risk performance, enabling your analysts to see how well your vendors actually implement and operate their risk management program. Deeper transparency yields greater accountability which produces better risk outcomes.
